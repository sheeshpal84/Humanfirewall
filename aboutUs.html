About HumanFirewall

Welcome to HumanFirewall, where security is no longer the sole responsibility of the InfoSec team. It's time for everyone to join in and contribute to a safer digital world.

For the first time ever, HumanFirewall makes it possible for every individual to take part in securing their organisation. With HumanFirewall, achieving security has never been easier.

We understand that the source of over 90% of all data breaches lies with humans. That's why we've developed a revolutionary solution that targets this issue head-on.

With our cutting-edge platform, you can protect against data breaches, minimize risk, and safeguard your business.

Gone are the days of relying on outdated security measures. HumanFirewall provides a proactive approach to cyber security, enabling organizations to stay one step ahead of potential threats.

Join the revolution and experience the benefits of a platform designed to mitigate human cyber risk for good!

4 Pillars/Objectives of HumanFirewall

Altering Human Psychology

Training every employee & making them suspicious in nature when receiving emails.

A culture of Reporting

While, it is important for the employees to have a reporting mechanism in place to remove the friction from reporting. It is also important to encourage the Culture of Reporting any suspicious email because ONE malicious email can send you back to DAY ZERO

Remediation

In real attack scenarios, you have the ability to quarantine or delete malicious emails from all inboxes once the reported email is confirmed malicious. Each reported email is instantly scanned for all signs of threats which reduces the time taken to action to under 30 seconds.

Human SOAR

The indicator of Compromise (IOC) of each reported malicious email gets communicated to all your current Cyber Security Investments, thereby identifying incoming threats and preventing any future penetration.
